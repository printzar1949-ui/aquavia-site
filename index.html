🌊 AquaVia

Water From Air

AquaVia is an innovative environmental project that aims to generate clean water from atmospheric air using condensation and purification technology.

💡 About the Project

The project extracts humidity from the air, converts it into water, purifies it, and stores it for safe use.

🌍 Objectives
  • Provide clean drinking water
  • Reduce water scarcity
  • Support sustainable environment

🌊 AquaVia

💡 About the Project

The project extracts humidity from the air, converts it into water, purifies it, and stores it for safe use. It is designed to help solve water scarcity problems using sustainable technology.

  • Provide clean drinking water
  • Reduce water scarcity
  • Support sustainable environment
