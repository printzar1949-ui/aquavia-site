About AquaVia

AquaVia Global Innovations is a visionary future-technology initiative founded by inventor Nezar Bachir Jdid — نزار بشير جديد, focused on atmospheric water generation, environmental sustainability, and clean infrastructure innovation.

Global Vision

Founded by inventor Nezar Bachir Jdid, AquaVia Global Innovations represents a vision for sustainable innovation combining environmental technology, natural nutrition, beauty concepts, and advanced industrial ideas. The mission is to transform creative concepts into impactful global solutions that support humanity, sustainability, and future investment opportunities.

The project aims to contribute to future global sustainability through innovative atmospheric water systems, renewable energy integration, and scalable environmental technologies designed for smart cities and remote regions.
